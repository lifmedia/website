# Digital PR Mastermind — LIF Media

Adapt the thread's "digital PR mastermind" tactic to a local video production
company. Goal: a working list of 20+ realistic outreach targets plus data angles
worth pitching. Uses `WebSearch` / `WebFetch` only — no paid tools.

## 1. Teach the strategy (data-gathering angles)
Pick angles that fit LIF Media and produce link-worthy assets:
- **Niche statistics**: video-marketing ROI / short-form trends, framed for NB SMBs.
- **Local data**: NB business growth, events, tourism, sectors LIF already serves
  (construction, healthcare, non-profit, hospitality, government).
- **Trends**: check Google Trends manually for "video production" + NB terms; ride risers.
- **Original data**: a small LIF survey/poll of local businesses on video usage — cheap,
  uniquely quotable, and a natural press hook.

## 2. Prospect 20+ outreach targets
Use `WebSearch` to build a list across these buckets (NB / Atlantic Canada focus):
- **Local & regional media**: CTV Atlantic, Global NB, Huddle, Acadie Nouvelle,
  Telegraph-Journal, CBC NB, local business journals.
- **Business orgs**: Greater Moncton Chamber of Commerce, 3+ Corporation, Opportunities
  NB, Ignite Fredericton, Venn Innovation, industry associations.
- **Partners & directories**: marketing agencies (white-label fit), event organizers,
  venues, wedding/corporate directories, "best videographers in Moncton" listicles.
- **Awards / features**: regional business/creative awards, "companies to watch" features.

For each target capture: name, URL, bucket, **why LIF fits / the angle**, contact path,
and status.

## 3. Output
Write `seo/digital-pr-targets.md`:
- A short section of 3–5 **data/story angles** (from step 1) with the asset each implies.
- A **table** of 20+ targets with the columns above.
- A suggested first-wave shortlist (5 highest-fit targets to pitch first).

## Guardrails
- Real, reachable targets only — verify each exists via `WebFetch`; no invented outlets.
- Pitches must reference real LIF work (UN, TSN, Wildcats, Pond-Deshpande, etc.).
- No spammy mass-outreach or link schemes; quality, relevance, genuine local value.

## Done means
`seo/digital-pr-targets.md` exists with 20+ verified targets, per-target angles, and a
prioritized first-wave shortlist.
