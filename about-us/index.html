About LIF Media

Your Strategic Video Production Partner in Moncton

We don't just hit record. We engineer growth for New Brunswick businesses through cinematic storytelling that resonates and converts.

Our Story

More Than Just
Video Production

We don't just shoot content, we frame meaning, one moment at a time.

LIF Media was founded by Jorge Melgar after noticing a clear gap in the Greater Moncton area: talented business owners and entrepreneurs were doing incredible work, but their marketing didn't reflect it. Jorge believes that every great business with great people and great culture deserves amazing video content, not generic clips, but real cinematic storytelling that captures who they are and why they matter. By helping companies across Moncton, Dieppe, Riverview, and throughout New Brunswick present themselves at their very best, we're not just producing videos, we're serving the community, helping people discover the outstanding local products, services, and professionals right in their own backyard.

Today, we act as strategic partners for businesses across Atlantic Canada, delivering organic, high-impact content that strips away the corporate veneer and connects on a deeply human level. The result? Thousands of authentic reactions, massive brand awareness, and a digital presence that drives real business growth.
