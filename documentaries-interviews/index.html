Why Brand Documentaries

People Don't Buy From Businesses. They Buy From People.

In a market flooded with quick-cut ads and disposable content, audiences are starving for something real. A brand documentary doesn't pitch, it connects. It takes your audience behind the curtain to meet the founders, the team, and the mission that drives your organization forward.

For businesses across Moncton, Dieppe, Riverview, and New Brunswick, a well-crafted documentary film is the most powerful trust-building asset you can own. It lives on your website, your social channels, and your sales presentations, working for you 24/7.

In a world of six-second attention spans, depth is the new differentiator. We slow down to capture the authenticity that builds lasting trust.

Production Formats

Designed for Depth

Whether you need a founder's origin story or an episodic LinkedIn series, we have a format built for your goals and timeline.

The Founder's Profile

A 3 to 5 minute cinematic film exploring the origin story, values, and vision of the company leader. The most powerful way to humanize your brand.

Timeline: ~2 Weeks

Client Case Studies

Turn your best success stories into powerful social proof. We interview your clients about their experience and deliver a polished testimonial video that sells for you.

Timeline: ~1 Week

Internal Training & Culture

High-quality educational content or recruitment films that showcase your company culture. Attract top talent in the Moncton market by showing what makes your workplace exceptional.

Timeline: ~1 Week

The Mini-Doc Series

Episodic content for YouTube or LinkedIn. Establish thought leadership with a recurring show and position your brand as the authority in your New Brunswick industry.

Timeline: ~1 Week per Episode

Questions About Brand Documentaries

Most brand documentaries take 2 to 3 weeks from discovery call to final delivery. A Founder's Profile typically takes about 2 weeks, while client case studies and culture films can be completed in about 1 week. Timeline depends on complexity, number of interview subjects, and location logistics across New Brunswick.

A commercial is a short, highly produced advertisement (15 to 60 seconds) designed to sell a specific product or service. A brand documentary is a longer-form piece (3 to 10 minutes) that tells the deeper story behind your business. Documentaries build trust and emotional connection, while commercials drive immediate action. Many businesses in Moncton use both.
