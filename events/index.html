How It Works

From Inquiry to Final Film

A simple, collaborative process that respects your time and ensures we capture exactly what matters to your brand.

1

Tell Us About Your Event

Fill out our quick form or call us directly. Share your event type, date, venue, and what success looks like. We'll respond within 24 hours with a custom proposal.

2

Strategy & Shot Planning

We hop on a pre-event call to walk through your agenda, identify must-capture moments, and align on the final deliverables. No surprises on event day.

3

We Show Up & Capture

Our crew arrives early, sets up discreetly, and captures every key moment, from crowd reactions to keynote speeches. If you've booked same-day socials, you'll have content going live before the event wraps.

4

Edit, Review & Deliver

We handle the edit with cinematic color grading, licensed music, and motion graphics, then deliver for your review. Unlimited revisions until it's perfect.
