## Month 1 — Own local "city + video production" (Gap G1)
Aspen ranks for `/moncton-video/`, `/halifax-video/` etc. with thin programmatic pages.
We beat them with genuinely local pages anchored in real nearby shoots.

- **Page:** `/video-production-dieppe/` — kw "video production Dieppe" (intent: local commercial). Anchor: nearest real Greater-Moncton project.
- **Page:** `/video-production-riverview/` — kw "video production Riverview NB".
- **Blog:** "What to look for in a Moncton video production company" — kw "Moncton video production company" (defends our home turf vs Aspen `/moncton-video/`).
- **Blog:** a project case-study post (any recent shoot) — keeps the blog moat fresh.

## Month 2 — Bilingual / French (Gap G2)
Atlas leans on `/fr/`. As an officially bilingual NB company we should own Francophone search.

- **Page:** `/fr/` — French homepage (kw "production vidéo Moncton / Nouveau-Brunswick").
